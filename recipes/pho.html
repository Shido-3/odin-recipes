Pho

Description

Beef broth soup with nice noodles. Beef slices, beef balls and beef tripes are added as protein.

I like to add sriracha for added kick and flavour.

Ingredients

  • Beef broth soup
    • Rice noodles
  • Beef balls
  • Beef tripe
  • Beef slices

Steps

  1. Heat up beef broth soup
  2. Boil Rice noodles to liking
  3. Cook all beef assortments
  4. Plate food, be careful, contents are very hot
  5. Add sriracha
